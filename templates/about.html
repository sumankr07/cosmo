About Cosmo Xclub

Cosmo Xclub is an exchange company involved in the Holiday Exchange program business. The concept entitles you to exchange your holiday home with the same or higher standard holiday home/villa/resort or hotel room for the same number of days.

Cosmopolitan XClub pvt ltd. is highly inspired by Sustainable Development Goals as set by the UNO and aims at working towards responsible tourism.
